Welcome to your site

I've set this site up for a layout site, but you can easily use it for anything you want. The sections below are just suggestions - move things around however you want!

You can put whatever you like in this welcome section; maybe a bit about you, maybe a spotlight of your best creations? Just try to avoid scrolling on this front page - you get two scroll bars next to each other because of the updates section and it ain't pretty.

Modifying the layout

  • The banner and footer images can be anything you want - just make sure it's minimum 1000px wide and has some decent height. Most backgorund images will work well.
  • When you change Title here to your site title you may need to move around the positions slightly to make things line up with each other and look neat.
  • Don't forget to update all the Neomail me signs to point to your own username!
  • If you want more or less links at the bottom you can change the width under .nav a { } to make them line up with the main content area.
  • Links are at the bottom so that they stay visible on the screen once you've clicked on them. I wouldn't recommend moving the links to the top because then your visitors will have to scroll up each time they want to navigate to a different section.
  • I've included two premade sections - maybe one for petpages and the other for lookups, maybe for anchored or column layouts, however you want it split!
